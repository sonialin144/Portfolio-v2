overview

Our project, MISA (Mock Interview Simulator Assistant), introduces an innovative approach to interview preparation by integrating artificial intelligence and virtual reality technologies. MISA's technical innovation centers on integrating cutting-edge AI, specifically the GPT-3 model by OpenAI, to dynamically engage and evaluate users in realistic interview simulations. Additionally, it utilizes the PICO headset’s interaction pack to monitor and assess non-verbal cues, such as facial expressions, eye movements, body language, and hand gestures, creating a highly immersive interview training experience.

The origins of this innovation can be traced back to the convergence of AI and VR and the need to address a significant challenge in the job market – the need to help job seekers improve their interview skills and boost their confidence. Traditional interview preparation methods, such as reading interview books or watching advice videos, often fall short in providing the kind of training and real-world experience that MISA aims to deliver. By integrating AI into a highly interactive VR environment, MISA seeks to transform the way individuals prepare for job interviews, addressing a previously underserved niche.

Visit Detroit AR

explore Detroit with augmented reality experiences

As part of the leadership for the university's XR club, we hold semesterly cohorts where students can collaborate on an XR project. This semester, Visit Detroit, Detroits official tourism bureau, contacted us about partnering with them on building a proof-of-concept AR mobile app for travel/tourism. We will building this with Unity and Google's ARCore Geospatial API.

Features we plan to include:

  • 3D models / animation showing up at a predefined location
  • 2D map overlap that syncs with real world location / Navigational AR
  • Text / Infographics appearing when approaching waypoint
  • Collectables at different locations

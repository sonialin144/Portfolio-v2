MISA: Mock Interview Simulator Assistant

2023 PICO Dev Jam

This project was created as a group of 2 and submitted to the 2023 PICO Dev Jam. Won 1st Prize and Best Non-Game App.

MISA is a virtual mock interview simulator assistant that helps job seekers ace their behavioral interviews! By using PICO's interaction pack and the OpenAI API, MISA can evaluate users' spoken answers and face and body language to give helpful and dynamic advice on how users can improve their interviews.

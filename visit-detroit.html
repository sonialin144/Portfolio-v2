overview

As part of the leadership for the university's XR club, we hold semesterly cohorts where students can collaborate on an XR project where they can learn valuable skills pertaining to XR development and project management. This semester, Visit Detroit, Detroit's official tourism bureau, contacted us about partnering with them on building a proof-of-concept AR mobile app for travel/tourism.

contribution

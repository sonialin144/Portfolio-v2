LeetRizz

An AI girlfriend that encourages you to do your LeetCode problems.

Devpost

This project was created for MHacks 2024. We decided to have fun with it and create something that addressed two heavily prevalent issues among CS students: finding a job and finding love. Although this was mostly built as a joke, I think I learned a lot since I don't do web development very much.

overview

3D Pigs in the Real World. Tontachi is an Augmented Reality (AR) based pet simulator featuring cute piggies called "Tons". Like Tamagotchi, these little piggies need your love and care to stay afloat because living in our world is tough! You can take Tons outside (ex. into your room) via AR for a walk, to pet, feed, or other fun activities. As you explore outside, you may find key items that eventually help your piggy grow.

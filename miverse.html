MIverse

learn about XR by interacting with characters in a cyberpunk universe!

We built this interactive PC/VR demo to showcase at our booth at the university's club fair. It serves as an engaging exhibit to help recruit more members for the XR club. We built both a PC version and VR version: the PC version is used for our outdoor exhibit where VR would not be feasible to use, and the VR version is for students to play and build additional features when they join the club as a series of Unity/Blender workshops.

Key Features:

  • Users can walk around and interact with various characters explaining the three categories of XR: AR, VR, MR.
  • Users can draw virtual graffiti on the walls in VR.
    • In the PC version, users can draw their designs on a piece of paper. Once we take a picture of the drawing on our phone and import it into a folder on our PC, our app will automatically convert it into a transparent png using a FloodFill algorithm and prompts the user to place their graffiti on the wall.
    • In the VR version, users can draw on the walls using their controllers.

CSE Simulator

EECS 498: Extended Reality and Society - Project 1

GitLab Repo

This VR experience was created as a learning project for the course EECS 498: Extended Reality and Society. The first half showcases a lot of the random requirements for the project to demonstrate our understanding. The second half displays our unique twist to the project. In homage of the legendary Slender: The Eight Pages, CSE Simulator has you running from a professor as you try to graduate by gathering EECS stickers. We built this in Unreal Engine 5 and used SketchFab and Quixel Content for all the models and materials.
